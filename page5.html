    Here Is Our Policy

    Here at Ancient Lakes, we want to offer you a fun filled and stress free experience day in and day out.A $100 dollar non refundable deposit per gun will be required at the time of booking to reserve yuor spot. We understand that life happens, and are willing to work with you on a per case basis.

    We have a five daycancelation policy that allows you to reschedule and put your deposit towards a future day in the field with us. Anything under the five day cancellation window will be forfeited from the deposit.

    Sometimes mother nature can be extremely unpredictable. This can cause passes and road closures. Tyson will be staying in contact with you in the days leading up to your trip so that everyone in your party can plan accordingly. We understand that sometimes weather can prohibit travel and hunting opportunities, if something like this happens Tyson will be in contact wit hyou about rescheduling as well as figuring out what other options are available.

    We do not do refunds. Our trips are typically very successful, but given the nature of hunting, there are no guarantees.

    We are still hunting wild game and while booking a trip with us will significantly increase your odds of having a successful trip, nothing is ever guaranteed. We encourage everyone to book a two day trip if possible to help allow yourself or group the best possible chance at making it a successful trip to remember!
